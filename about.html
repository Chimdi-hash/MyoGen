// Workflow

The MYOGEN Journey

Step 01
Connect Your Wallet

Use MetaMask or any EVM-compatible wallet. MYOGEN automatically configures GenLayer Bradbury Testnet (Chain ID 961) and registers your address on-chain.

Step 02
Navigate to Study Room

The Study page presents an intuitive terminal-inspired interface with quick-select popular terms and a clean input field for custom queries.

Step 03
Sign the Transaction

Clicking "Study" triggers MetaMask to sign a transaction calling MYOGEN's study_term() Intelligent Contract function on GenLayer.

Step 04
AI Validators Analyze

5 GenLayer validators independently call an LLM with your term. Using Optimistic Democracy, they reach consensus on the most accurate physiological explanation.

Step 05
Learn & Visualize

The result — definition, key facts, clinical relevance, related terms — is displayed with an animated anatomical visualization. Study the next term immediately with no timeouts.

// FAQ

Common Questions

What is GenLayer and why is MYOGEN built on it?
+
GenLayer is a blockchain that allows smart contracts ("Intelligent Contracts") to call large language models and access the web directly from on-chain execution. MYOGEN uses this to let AI validators analyze muscle physiology terms and reach consensus on accurate explanations — something impossible with traditional Solidity contracts.
Do I need real money to use MYOGEN?
+
No. MYOGEN runs on GenLayer's Bradbury Testnet, which uses GEN testnet tokens that have no real-world value. You may need a small amount of GEN for gas — check the GenLayer faucet at the Discord server for free testnet tokens.
Why do I need to sign a transaction just to look up a term?
+
MYOGEN is decentralized — meaning every query is a real blockchain transaction that goes to on-chain validators. This ensures the explanation you receive is validated by multiple independent AI nodes and permanently recorded on-chain, not served from a centralized server that could censor or manipulate results.
Can I study multiple terms back to back?
+
Absolutely. MYOGEN is designed for continuous learning. After viewing a result, click "Study Another Term" and immediately enter the next one. The contract caches previously validated terms so repeat queries are instant. No timeout errors will interrupt your study session.
How accurate are the AI-generated explanations?
+
MYOGEN uses GenLayer's Optimistic Democracy consensus — 5 validators independently query LLMs and compare results. Only responses that pass the equivalence principle are accepted on-chain. This multi-validator approach significantly reduces hallucination and single-model bias. However, always cross-reference with primary literature for clinical decisions.
What terms does MYOGEN cover?
+
MYOGEN covers muscle physiology, anatomy, kinesiology, exercise science, biomechanics, and sports medicine. This includes structural terms (sarcomere, fascicle), proteins (actin, myosin, troponin), physiological processes (cross-bridge cycle, hypertrophy), neural components (motor unit, NMJ), fiber types, and clinical pathologies. Any out-of-scope term will be flagged by the AI validators.
